# Retail Sales Performance & Time-Series Analytics 📊

## 📝 Project Overview
This repository contains an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Time-Series exploration of a major retail **Superstore**. The core objective of this project is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights. By leveraging Python's data science ecosystem, the analysis uncovers hidden sales patterns, seasonal trends, and key drivers of profitability to aid data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
* Identify top-performing product categories and regional markets.
* Analyze the impact of discount strategies on overall profitability.
* Discover seasonal sales trends to optimize future inventory and marketing campaigns.

## 🛠️ Tools & Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python 3.9+
* **Data Manipulation:** Pandas, NumPy
* **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly
* **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook

## 📊 Key Business Insights
* **Revenue Growth:** Identified a consistent upward trend in sales from mid-2019 through the end of 2020.
* **Top Performers:** The *Technology* sector is the highest-revenue driver, with *Chairs* and *Phones* dominating the sub-category sales.
* **Seasonality:** Pronounced sales spikes occur annually during November and December, reflecting strong holiday demand.
* **Profitability Threshold:** Found a strong positive correlation between Sales and Profit; however, applying discounts beyond **~20%** significantly erodes profit margins.
